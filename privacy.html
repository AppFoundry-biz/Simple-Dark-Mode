Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 25, 2026

Overview

Simple Dark Mode is a browser extension that toggles dark mode on websites using CSS color inversion. Your privacy is important to us — this extension collects no personal data.

Data Collection

This extension does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information. It does not use analytics, tracking, or any third-party services.

Local Storage

The extension stores your dark mode preference (on/off) per website using your browser's local storage (chrome.storage.local and localStorage). This data never leaves your browser and is only used to remember your settings.

Permissions

  • activeTab — To apply dark mode to the current tab when you click the extension icon.
  • storage — To save your per-site dark mode preferences locally in your browser.
  • scripting — To inject the dark mode CSS into web pages.

Third-Party Services

This extension does not communicate with any external servers or third-party services.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy is updated, the changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
